Questions
Frequently Asked
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +
Yes. You can upgrade at any time and the new plan takes effect immediately. Downgrading takes effect at the next billing cycle.
Do unused pattern downloads roll over? +
Downloads reset each month and do not roll over. We recommend planning your downloads at the start of each month to make the most of your plan.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and major digital wallets. All payments are processed securely.
Are the manufacturer contact details verified? +
Yes. Every manufacturer in our database has been personally verified by our team. We regularly update contact details and add new verified partners.
What is a custom pattern request? (Pro Elite) +
Pro Elite members can request patterns for specific garments that aren't in our library. Our team will create them and add them to your account within 5–7 business days.
