How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
  • To provide and maintain our services — processing your subscription, granting access to premium content, and delivering pattern files and spec sheets
  • To process your subscriptions and payments — verifying transactions via Razorpay and managing your billing cycle
  • To send you updates and marketing communications — only with your explicit consent; you can opt out at any time
  • To improve our services and user experience — analysing usage patterns to enhance the platform
  • To provide customer support — responding to your queries via email, WhatsApp, or phone as per your plan
  • To protect against fraudulent or illegal activity — monitoring for unusual account activity
We will never use your personal information for purposes beyond what is described in this policy without your explicit consent.
03

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:
  • Service providers who assist in our operations — such as Razorpay for payment processing and Supabase for secure data storage
  • Professional advisers and regulatory authorities — when required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
  • Business transfers — in the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction
🔒   Payments are processed securely by Razorpay — PCI DSS compliant
04

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:
  • Encrypted storage of user data via Supabase with row-level security policies
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Secure password hashing — your password is never stored in plain text
  • Payment data handled exclusively by Razorpay — we never store card numbers or CVVs
  • Regular review of our data collection and storage practices
However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information to improve and analyse our service. The types of cookies we use include:
  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function, including maintaining your login session
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g. pages visited, time spent)
  • Preference cookies — used to remember your settings and preferences across visits
You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of our service may not function correctly, including staying logged in.
