Common Questions
Before You Write
How quickly do you respond to enquiries? +
We respond to all enquiries within 24 business hours Monday–Friday. Messages sent on weekends or after 6 PM IST will be responded to the next business day.
How do I get a quote for custom pattern making? +
Send us a message using the form above with details about your garment category, design complexity, and quantities needed. We'll send you a quote within 24 hours.
Can I visit your office to discuss my project? +
Yes, in-person consultations are available at our Bijwasan, Delhi office, but only by prior appointment. Please send us an email to schedule a visit.
I have a subscription issue — who do I contact? +
Select "Subscription / Billing" in the subject dropdown and describe your issue. We'll resolve billing and subscription queries within 12 business hours.
Do you work with brands outside Delhi? +
Yes! We serve brands across India. All pattern files are delivered digitally, and our manufacturer connections cover Delhi NCR. Remote consultations are available via email and WhatsApp (premium members).
